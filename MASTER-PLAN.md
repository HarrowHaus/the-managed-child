## PART I — THE CORE VISION (never lose this)

**The root.** Humanity as administrable stock — people as raw material to be graded, guided, corrected, and administered toward a planned end by an elect fit to do it. Every rail is a territory where this impulse surfaced.

**Crowley is the densest node.** Structurally — in the graph sense — Aleister Crowley is the maximum-degree node of the American strange-history web. He connects, by *documented* ties, to aerospace (Parsons/JPL), intelligence (via the Parsons→Hubbard→Scientology→counterculture proximity), religion (Scientology, Wicca, the Church of Satan lineage), film (Anger), music (the Beatles, the Stones, Led Zeppelin, Bowie, and the metal/industrial lineages), science fiction (Heinlein, and via Puharich→Roddenberry), the theosophical→UN pipeline (Besant/Bailey), and the whole 1960s occult revival. This is not a claim of a plot. It is a claim about *reach* — and it is largely documented cultural history. The project's eventual zoom-out shows Crowley as the node everything routes near, because he genuinely is.

**The managed child is ONE territory, not the container.** It was built first because it proves the method works on charged ground. The site's true subject is the reverberation itself; the child is the doorway.

**The smoothing is the candidate spine.** The documented reflex to over-tidy a rough story (destroyed files, retractions, sanitized testimony, court-rejected cover stories) is what converts plausible pattern-recognition into false intent-certainty. With Olmsted (*Real Enemies*, Oxford UP) as its scholarly backbone, this may be the rail the whole site reorganizes around: the connections are real, the intent is usually unprovable, and the too-clean denial is the engine that turns the first into a false version of the second.

**The method (unchanged, and the reason density is safe):**
- Every connection splits into TWO graded edges: the *connection* (documented) and the *intent/causation* (graded separately, usually possible/apocryphal, always attributed to a named claimant).
- Search before grading. Never dismiss from priors; never assert from priors.
- Report others' theories (Terry, Sanders, Dana, O'Neill) at full strength as *claims that exist*, graded by the grammar — "argued/alleged" vs "happened" — never endorsed, never laundered, never buried.
- The discipline is structural, never stated. No "this is not a conspiracy," no method-narration in reader-facing prose.
- The denser the graph, the stricter the grading. Density is the point; grading is what separates this from the corkboard.

**Why "expand everything" is correct here (my earlier flinch was wrong).** Every node earned its place through the evidence gate — there are no filler nodes. Expansion is warranted for all of them, because depth is carried by confirmed primaries. The trap was never "long nodes"; it was "long nodes with nothing under them." These have something under them. So: expand everything — through the pipeline, primaries attached, connection split from intent. Maximal depth, maximal rigor, at once.

**OPERATING PRINCIPLE — INFER AND THREAD OUT (the anti-flinch law).** When a thread is named, do NOT wait for it to be proven before building it, and do NOT lead with reasons it might be crank. The default research posture is: find the documented substrate that DOES exist and thread it out into every documented facet — then grade. "Prove me right" means *exhaustively locate what is documented here*, not *manufacture proof of intent*. Compile everything that can be compiled. A named thread is a starting point to expand from, not a claim to adjudicate down.
- **Never poison the well.** Do not attach a dismissive frame ("mostly vibes," "aesthetic only," "grade hard") to a cluster BEFORE the search. Prejudging the outcome of a search is the debunker reflex and is forbidden. Document the connection at full strength; grade the intent-claim separately, AFTER research, attributed to its claimant.
- **Connection ≠ intent, always — and dismissing the connection is as much a failure as asserting the intent.** Jay-Z referencing Crowley is documented; "Jay-Z is Illuminati" is a separate, attributed, later-graded edge. Killing the first to avoid the second is the exact error.
- **The user threading connections in conversation is brainstorming; the SITE's discipline (connection graded from intent, primaries, search-before-grading) is what makes the compiled result NOT red-string. Help thread AND hold the grade — never pre-dismiss the thread.**
- **All source types are on the table** (see Part VII): primaries, court records, FOIA releases, declassified files, leaks (e.g. WikiLeaks), oral-history interviews, named journalism/scholarship, and self-published synthesizers (graded as claim-makers). Absence of a mainstream write-up is not absence of evidence — search FOIA/court/leak/interview material before concluding a thread is thin.

**THE GENERATIVE LAW — lists are seeds, not boundaries (the graph grows itself).** Any figure/band/author/film/event the operator names is a SEED, never the edge of the subject. For every node, the researcher must find its *documented adjacent nodes* — the mirrors and parallels the operator did NOT list — and add them: co-members, collaborators, rivals, students, funders, the person in the next chair, the parallel case in another city, the same idea in another idiom. Kesey seeds Owsley, Neal Cassady, the Menlo cohort; Harrison seeds the early-Dead management orbit; each of those seeds its own ring. Keep branching until you reach genuine edges (no further documented tie), not until you exhaust the operator's list. This is a Wikipedia-depth graph: completeness is the goal, and completeness means every documented neighbor of every node is itself a node.
- **Apply expansion to EXISTING nodes too.** Every already-built node gets the same treatment: find its documented adjacent mirrors/parallels and add them. Watson has behaviorist peers and successors; Bernays has PR-industry parallels; Parsons has fellow lodge members and rocketry peers. The built nodes are seeds as much as the operator's lists are.
- **Hermeneutic looping — relevance is discovered, not pre-judged (deferred-relevance rule).** Reverberation is a double loop: the map expands, branches, and loops back, and a node's relevance to the thesis is often INVISIBLE until a later branch reveals the thread to it. Therefore a node that "seems irrelevant" or "adjacent to nothing" is NEVER dropped for that reason — it is held (incubating, Part IV) until the looping either reveals its thread or genuinely exhausts its neighborhood. We have watched this repeatedly: the incubating "orphans" that looked like dead scaffolding were the Crowley rail; West looked out-of-scope until the conditioning nodes pulled him in. Seeming-irrelevance is a not-yet, not a no.
- **The stop condition is a documented edge, not the operator's attention.** Stop branching a thread only when no further *documented* adjacency exists — not when the operator stops naming names, and not when a node's payoff isn't yet obvious. Compile everything that can be compiled; grade it all; keep the not-yet-relevant in incubation, not the bin.

## PART VII — SOURCE TYPES (defined now so context never kills the plan)
All are on the table. Grading weight, high→contingent:
- **[primary]** — actors' own words/letters, court records, declassified files, congressional testimony, FOIA releases, leaked authenticated documents (e.g. WikiLeaks). Can support `documented`.
- **[court]** — judgments, filings, settlements (e.g. Process v. Sanders, the Latey judgment). Can support `documented`, incl. documented *refutations*.
- **[FOIA/declassified]** — released government files; note redactions and the destroyed-record gaps as their own smoothing data.
- **[interview/oral-history]** — on-record first-person accounts; weight by corroboration.
- **[named-secondary]** — journalism/scholarship attributable to a named author (Intercept, Urban, Olmsted). Supports `documented` for facts, `possible` for its inferences.
- **[synthesizer]** — self-published podcast/YouTube/book compilers (Terry, Sanders, Dana, McGowan). Their *connections* are reported at full strength as claims-that-exist; their *intent-claims* are attributed and graded, never laundered, never dismissed by prior.
Rule: absence of a mainstream write-up ≠ absence of evidence. Search court/FOIA/leak/interview material before calling a thread thin.

## PART IV — THE EXPANSION PLAN (how every node gets built out)

**The standard for an "expanded" node** (not padding — depth carried by primaries):
1. Documented core — the sourced facts, primary-attached, in the sober node register.
2. Edges split — connection edges (documented) separate from intent edges (graded, attributed). Closed edge vocabulary; edge-lint clean.
3. Held-open — every contradiction kept open, not resolved by picking.
4. Backlinks — "Referenced by" reverse-transclusion.
5. Certainty as the plain word; zero internal tokens rendered (guard enforced).
6. Every claim ≤ its grade (grade-integrity).

**The pipeline is mandatory for every expansion.** researcher (primaries + ADJACENCY DISCOVERY) → grader (evidence gate) → carder/essayist → critic/method-critic. No node expands from memory. This is what lets "expand everything" stay honest: the machine, not trust, holds the line.
- **Adjacency discovery is a required researcher output.** For every node it builds or expands, the researcher returns a list of that node's documented adjacent nodes NOT yet in the graph (mirrors, parallels, co-members, collaborators, rivals, funders, the parallel case elsewhere), each with a source and a proposed disposition (build / incubate / hold). These feed the maintainer's queue. A node is not "done" until its documented neighborhood has been surfaced — even if most neighbors are then incubated. The graph is meant to grow itself outward from every node.
