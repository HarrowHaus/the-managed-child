### Anchor 1 — Charles Davenport & the Eugenics Record Office (ERO), Cold Spring Harbor, 1910–1939: the American movement's institutional engine

### Anchor 7 — *Buck v. Bell* (1927): the documented KEYSTONE act — the court primary is gold
**Connection [PRIMARY — the Supreme Court opinion]:** ***Buck v. Verbatim, load-bearing:
> "The principle that sustains compulsory vaccination is broad enough to cover cutting the Fallopian tubes."

Oklahoma*, 316 U.S. 535 (1942), and "distinguished… out of existence" (G. Edward White), but never expressly reversed. [PRIMARY] the opinion — Justia (274 U.S. 200): https://supreme.justia.com/cases/federal/us/274/200/ (note: 403 to automated fetch in-session; the opinion text is also at Cornell LII and https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Buck_v._Bell — verify the two quotes verbatim against the U.S. Reports at build).
