## The PRIMARY — *The Externalisation of the Hierarchy* (Bailey / "the Tibetan," 1957)

**Full title:** *The Externalisation of the Hierarchy* (US spelling on some editions: *Externalization*).
**Author / byline:** **Alice A. Bailey**, presented as the amanuensis of **"the Tibetan," later named Djwhal Khul** — Bailey's stated practice across the "Tibetan books" was telepathic dictation.
**Composition:** the constituent letters/instructions written **1919–1949** (the "Tibetan" contact began **November 1919**); *Externalisation* gathers the Hierarchy-emergence material.
**Publisher / date:** **Lucis Publishing Company** (New York) / Lucis Press (London), **first published 1957**, posthumously (Bailey died 15 December 1949). Reprinting financed through a revolving fund under Lucis Trust; no royalties; copyrights held by Lucis Trust.
**Full text (verbatim-check hosts):** Internet Archive `TheExternalizationOfTheHierarchyBailey` (PDF + `_djvu.txt`); lucistrust.org online-books ("Section Four — Stages in the Externalisation of the Hierarchy," Parts 1–2); bailey.it `/files/The-Externalization-of-the-Hierarchy.pdf`; libracentre.com PDF.

### Verbatim doctrine [PRIMARY — text, cross-checked against the Internet Archive djvu full text]
*(quoted short, per fair-use; consistent across the archive full-text and the publisher e-text)*

- **The externalisation itself — the Masters step into the open:** *"the Hierarchy will no longer be hidden and unknown, but will be recognised as present upon the physical plane."* This is the book's title-thesis in one line: the hidden elect ceases to be hidden and works openly among humanity.
- **The Masters "breaking Their long silence":** the Masters of the Wisdom, whose attention is *"directed or focussed at this time on human affairs,"* are described as *"preparing to break Their long silence and may appear later among men."*
- **The Plan (administered from above):** the work is oriented to *"the purpose which the Masters know and serve"* and to letting *"the Plan of Love and Light work out."* The "Plan" is a divine blueprint for humanity's evolution, **known to the initiated few and executed through their instruments** — the administrative claim in esoteric dress.
- **The New Group of World Servers (the instruments):** it was the need for *"a bridging group which would be neither entirely negative nor entirely positive which prompted some of the Masters … to form the New Group of World Servers"* — intermediaries between the masses and the Hierarchy, the disciples through whom the Plan is worked out. (This confirms the coinage `four-idiom-through-line` had **held open** as "not yet verified against her texts.")
- **The reappearance of the Christ:** the material anticipates the return of **the Christ** and *"certain of His great disciples, the Masters of the Wisdom"* into world affairs — *"May Christ return to Earth"* — the eschatological head of the externalisation. (Elaborated in Bailey's companion volume *The Reappearance of the Christ*, 1948.)
- **The Great Invocation (the mantram of the Plan):** *"Let the Forces of Light bring illumination to mankind … Let the Spirit of Peace be spread abroad …"* — the invocation Bailey issued (from 1945) for daily group recitation to hasten the Plan; still the signature mantram of Triangles/World Goodwill.

**The single load-bearing line for the thesis:** the whole book is the argument that *humanity's development is administered by a superior initiated few, working a Plan from above, now preparing to administer it openly.* That is `the-root` ("humanity as administrable stock … administered toward a planned end by an elect fit to do it") and `rule-by-trained-elite` ("a trained guardian minority who shape the many") **restated almost word-for-word in the esoteric idiom** — the most explicit such articulation in the corpus.

### The institutional record — Lucis Trust [documented / WELD; already a node on disk]

The name honours Blavatsky's Theosophical journal *Lucifer* ("light-bearer") — **documented name, not a cabal signal.**

### The UN tie — mundane fact vs. conspiracy claim [R-8 core]

- **DOCUMENTED / mundane (WELD-able as a fact about Lucis Trust's activity, NOT as UN control):** **World Goodwill / Lucis Trust holds NGO consultative status with the UN Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC)** and is "recognised by the Office of Public Information at the United Nations as a Non-Governmental Organisation," represented at regular UN briefings in New York and Geneva. **As of 31 December 2024, ~6,494 NGOs hold active ECOSOC consultative status** — it confers advisory participation, **not** operational control.
