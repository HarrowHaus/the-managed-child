## The PRIMARIES

### 1. *The Secret Teachings of All Ages* (self-published 1928) [PRIMARY — the "Great Book"]
**Full title (long form):** *An Encyclopedic Outline of Masonic, Hermetic, Qabbalistic and Rosicrucian Symbolical Philosophy* — commonly *The Secret Teachings of All Ages.*
**Author:** **Manly P. Hall** (written in his mid-twenties; illustrated by **J. Augustus Knapp**).
**Publication:** self-published, **1928**, San Francisco (the "King Solomon" / subscriber folio editions); ~200 oversized pages, ~50 chapters, each "the equivalent of an entire short book." **Copyright not renewed** → public domain; full scans on Internet Archive; notably included in the **CIA's Abbottabad-compound reading-list scans** (a marker of its documented cultural reach).
- **What it IS:** a vast compendium/encyclopedia of Western esoterica — Hermeticism, Qabbalah, Rosicrucianism, Freemasonry, Pythagoras, the Mysteries — presented as a **single perennial wisdom-tradition** transmitted by initiates across the ages. It is the source-of-record for the symbol-set and the seedbed of the "one perennial wisdom, guarded by an elect" frame Hall carries into *The Secret Destiny*.
- **Register note (R-8):** Hall presents the perennial tradition as REAL. Under the new bar this is includable **as Hall's articulated belief** (WELD on "Hall wrote/believed this"); it is **not** granted `documented` standing as a truth-claim about a literal unbroken brotherhood.

### 2. The Philosophical Research Society (PRS), Los Angeles — founded 1934 [PRIMARY — the institution exists]
**Founded:** **1934**, by Manly P. Hall, as an American nonprofit "to promote the study of the world's wisdom literature, philosophy, comparative religion, mysticism and metaphysics." Campus at **3910 Los Feliz Boulevard, Los Angeles** (Griffith Park / Los Feliz). Hall was its **first president and remained so until his death in 1990.** Still operating (library, lectures, press, bookstore).
- **What it IS (WELD):** a real, dated, still-extant institution — the enacting body of Hall's project (lectures, a research library of esoterica, publishing). The PRS is the *institutional* face of the articulation, exactly as the Abbey was for Crowley's pedagogy (documented practice, not merely a theory). **No `prs` node exists on disk — do NOT invent one; report in prose (the builder may create it as a separate act).**

### 3. *The Secret Destiny of America* (1944) [PRIMARY — the articulation, verbatim-checked in substance]
**Author / date:** **Manly P. Hall, 1944** (developed from a lecture Hall delivered c. 1942; sources associate the address with Carnegie Hall). Full text online (holybooks; yamaguchy.com library mirror).
- **The thesis, in Hall's frame [PRIMARY — content]:** the founding of the United States was **planned centuries in advance** by initiates of the ancient Mystery schools; the line runs **Plato's *Republic* (the commonwealth / the philosopher-king) → Bacon's *New Atlantis* (the Philosophic Empire) → the Masonic/Rosicrucian founders.** "Men bound by a secret oath to labor in the cause of world democracy decided that in the American colonies … they would plant the roots of a new way of life."

- **The Baconian core [PRIMARY — content, load-bearing for the edges]:** "**Bacon quickly realized that here in the new world was the proper environment for the accomplishment of his great dream, the establishment of the philosophic empire.**" And: "**Bacon did not play a lone hand; he was the head of a secret society including in its membership the most brilliant intellectuals of his day.**" Hall reads *New Atlantis* as "a coded blueprint" for "the one empire that will rule them all: the Philosophic Empire." **This is the exact `bacon-francis` / `new-atlantis` material the project already grades WELD for the political trunk — voiced here in the esoteric key.**
