# THE MANAGED CHILD — book outline (Tier 1)
*The flagship. A traversal of the graph with a thesis, in the sober register (not the essay voice). Each part draws on the nodes named; each chapter is a stub to expand from `data/nodes/**` + `map/lineage-map.md`.*

**Governing rule:** lead on Thesis A (documented sociology) as fact; present Thesis B (the esoteric current) as the root's self-aware confession, never as cause. Make the denominator concession early and out loud.

---

## Part I — The Root
1. **Humanity as administrable stock.** The deepest object: the conviction that people are material for an elect to grade and guide. Nodes: `the-root`, `rule-by-trained-elite`.
2. **The trunk.** Plato's guardians and noble lie → Bacon's Salomon's House → the Fabian machine. Nodes: `rule-by-trained-elite`, `fabian-society`, `permeation`, `coefficients-club`, `webb-sidney`.

## Part II — The Confession (the esoteric register)
3. **The seed.** 1904: the Crowned & Conquering Child — *and* "few & secret… rule the many" in the same text. Nodes: `liber-al-vel-legis`, `crowley-aleister`, `aeon-of-the-child`.
4. **The hinge and the four idioms.** Besant carries the disposition from Fabianism into Theosophy; the through-line Aeon → World-Teacher → Hierarchy → Nine. Nodes: `besant-annie`, `order-of-the-star`, `bailey-alice`, `puharich-andrija`, `four-idiom-through-line`.

## Part III — The Enactment (the sociological register)
5. **Measuring the child.** Hall invents adolescence; the eugenic measurement rail; the loop James→Hall→Terman. Nodes: `hall-g-stanley`, `terman-lewis`, `galton-francis`.
6. **Conditioning the child.** Watson from lab to nursery to agency desk. Nodes: `watson-john-b`, `jwt`, `psychological-care`.
7. **Engineering consent.** The Lippmann–Dewey debate; the manufacture of consent. Nodes: `lippmann-walter`, `bernays-edward`, `lippmann-dewey-debate`.
8. **The denominator.** Who *actually* built it — Mann, Cubberley, Holt, Gesell, Spock — and why that confirms the root instead of sinking it. Node: `managed-child` + `research/` denominator material.

## Part IV — The Mechanism
9. **Reverberation, not conspiracy.** How ideas travel without coordination; worked-off vs. same-field. Node: `method-stance`.
10. **Liberation as the first step.** Nisbet; dissolve the family buffer, then management fills the vacuum. Nodes: `double-parentage`, `krishnamurti-custody`.

## Part V — The Lifespan
11. **From the nursery to the toy aisle.** 1984 and the toy-cartoon complex. Node: `fcc-1984`.
12. **The child grown up.** The continuum into adult esoteric cinema; the Nine reaches Roddenberry. Nodes: `lifespan-continuum`, `roddenberry-gene`, `campbell-joseph`, `vogler-christopher`.

## Coda — Either Way
13. **What the record shows, and where it stops.** The honest boundaries; the grading as the argument.
