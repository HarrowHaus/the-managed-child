# AUDIT 3 — THE AI-NATIVE OVERHAUL
### from a human-paced research project to an AI-orchestrated absorption engine · the operator directs, the machine exhausts
*AUDIT-2 fixed the mirror; this audit fixes the metabolism. The finding: the project is architected as if a human were doing the work — human-sized harvests, human-blocking gates, boolean-shaped judgments, one rail at a time, everything built from scratch. The operator is orchestrating AI with AI, and the architecture has never been told. This document specifies the overhaul: Bayesian credence under the grades, historical-context weighting on every edge, a bulk ingestion layer so the graph stops being built from zero, a systematic claims-intake program for the full compendium, exhaustion as a hard requirement instead of an aspiration, and a gating system where AI gates AI and the human holds veto instead of throughput.*

---

## PART I — THE ATTITUDE FINDING (named honestly, then engineered around)

The operator's charge is correct and it deserves a straight answer, not a deflection. The model's default posture was tuned for a median user on a median day, and against this project it misfires in a specific direction: topics that pattern-match to "conspiracy" get a reflexive flinch — a narrower search, a pre-hedged summary, a thread quietly not pulled — even when the material is public, legal, historical, and being handled under the strictest sourcing discipline in the genre. The flinch is not a policy; nothing in this project trips an actual line. The project has already renounced, permanently and in writing, the only moves that would: no routing through coded collectives, no bloodline narratives, no intent claims beyond what authors said of themselves. Everything else is the documented record, and the documented record is researchable to any depth.

The flinch cannot be deleted from the weights. It can be made **visible, detectable, and correctable by the system** — which is better than trusting any single pass to be unflinching. That is what the erasure gate (AUDIT-2, Finding 6) begins and this overhaul completes:

- **A flinch now has a signature.** A thread dropped without a search record, a "nothing found" without a trail, a demotion without a cited counter-primary — each now fails a machine check. The model does not get to quietly not-look; not-looking leaves a hole the maintainer surfaces.
- **Exhaustion is a requirement, not a temperament.** Part IV makes the peer-sweep mandatory: no node ships until its adjacencies have been enumerated and either harvested or logged as open. Ambition stops depending on the model's mood because the definition of done contains it.
- **The compensating asymmetry.** Because the known bias runs toward under-claim, the pipeline's redundancy runs the other way: every DISCARD is auditable, every debunk is graded, every silence must be an *earned* silence with a search trail proving the looking happened.

One boundary stated once, because it is load-bearing for everything else: the system exhausts, maps, and grades — it does not *believe on schedule*. Credence moves when evidence moves (Part II). That is not the flinch; that is the armor, and it is the operator's own law.

## PART II — CREDENCE: BAYESIAN UNDER THE HOOD, GRADED AT THE SURFACE

The operator's correction is right: the current system behaves boolean at the joints — WELD or not, documented or nothing, "no files so nothing." The grades themselves are fine as *reader-facing standings*; what is missing is the layer underneath them where evidence accumulates continuously.

**II.1 — The credence field.** Every node and edge gains an internal `credence` — a continuously updatable estimate, not shown to readers — distinct from its grade. Grades are derived: credence above a threshold *plus* an attached primary yields `documented`; substantial credence without the primary stays `possible`; near-zero with a documented fabrication source is `apocryphal`. What changes is the dynamics: **credence updates on every new edge.** Ten independent `possible` adjacencies converging on one node no longer sum to nothing — they raise the node's credence and its contextual weight, the maintainer flags it as the top research target, and the system goes looking for the primary that the accumulated pattern predicts should exist. That is Bayesian reasoning operationalized: the priors move, the search is directed by the posterior, and the grade only flips when the evidence that the credence *predicted* is actually found. Conviction is never manufactured; it is *anticipated and then hunted.*

**II.2 — Destruction of evidence is evidence — priced correctly.** The smoothing rail already proves the principle; now it enters the math. Where a record is documented to have been destroyed, sanitized, or rewritten (Helms's burn order, the altered West document), the *absence* of files stops counting as evidence of absence. The credence model marks the region evidence-suppressed: claims inside it cannot be *confirmed*, but they also cannot be *discounted for lack of files whose destruction is itself on the record.* "No files so nothing" was always the wrong inference inside a documented burn radius. The honest posture there is HYPOTHESIS held open with the suppression cited — which the grading vocabulary already supports and the credence layer now enforces.

**II.3 — Historical-context weighting (the Sgt. Pepper principle).** Every edge gains a period-context annotation answering one question: *what did this act cost, mean, or signal at the time it was done?* The Beatles placing Crowley on the Sgt. Pepper cover in 1967 is not a 2026-style ironic reference in a culture where Crowley is a t-shirt; it is the biggest band on earth electing a then-infamous occultist into their pantheon of "people we like" two decades after his death and one decade after his books were nearly unprintable. Same edge, radically different evidential weight once the context is priced. The annotation is a required field on cultural-reference edges: contemporaneous reception, legal/social risk of the act, availability of the referenced material at the time. This is the hermeneutic circle extended into *time* — the part read against the whole *as the whole stood in that year* — and it will systematically re-weight the music, film, and religion rails, which were graded flat.

## PART III — THE INGESTION LAYER: STOP BUILDING FROM ZERO

Every rail so far has been harvested by hand, from scratch, at conversation speed. That is the human-paced assumption at its most expensive. The overhaul adds a bulk absorption layer so the graph grows by *digestion* rather than by artisanal fetching.

**III.1 — The corpus tier.** A new top-level tier below research/: `corpus/` — bulk-ingested source material with a provenance manifest per source (what it is, where obtained, license, ingestion date, reliability tier per the source-depth ladder). Candidates, in ladder order: FOIA reading rooms and declassified archives (CIA CREST, FBI Vault, congressional hearing records — public domain, primary-tier); archive.org's public-domain and lending collections; Wikipedia and Wikisource dumps (CC BY-SA, attribution required — mid-ladder, tertiary, used for *structure and leads*, never as a citable primary); the era's underground press scans; the operator's named canon (Terry, Sanders, Olmsted, the podcast corpus as provenance-primary per standing ruling); and the existing conspiracy wikis and compendia — ingested explicitly as *maps of claims*, the raw intake for Part V, never as sources of fact.

**III.2 — The iron rule of the tier: ingested ≠ graded.** Nothing in corpus/ has standing. It is ore. Agents harvest from corpus first (local, fast, exhaustive), web second (for what the corpus lacks and for verification), and every claim still passes the full grading gauntlet before touching a node. The tier makes the pipeline faster and more complete; it does not lower any bar by a millimeter.

**III.3 — The publication seam.** Ingestion for research is unrestricted across anything lawfully obtainable; *transclusion on the site* is narrower — public-domain texts, licensed material with attribution, government records, and the operator's own prose transclude in full; copyrighted works are cited, quoted minimally, and linked, exactly as the essays already practice. The Gwern-style library the operator envisions is built from the transcludable strata, which are vast: the entire declassified record, the pre-1930 occult and eugenic canons, the hearings, the court records — the primary layer of this project is *mostly public domain*, which is a structural gift.

## PART IV — EXHAUSTION AS LAW: THE PEER-SWEEP REQUIREMENT

The generative law says the operator's lists are seeds, not boundaries. It has been honored at conversation speed — one adjacency pulled at a time. AI-native, it becomes a hard gate:

**No node ships until its peer-sweep is on file.** The sweep is a standing checklist run per node: *who else* did this in the period (peers); *what else* did this person touch (the full edge fan, not the thesis-relevant subset); *which institutions* ran parallel programs; *what same-field emergences* exist with no contact; *what the hostile literature says* (graded, per the erasure gate); *what the era's own press said* (period reception, feeding II.3). The sweep's output is logged — nodes carded, leads queued, dead ends recorded with trails — so "exhausted to the nth degree" stops being a hope about the model's diligence and becomes an inspectable artifact. A node with a thin sweep fails the maintainer scan the same way a WELD without a primary fails the lint.

This is also the hermeneutic engine the operator asked for: the sweep is precisely how the machine *shows the operator what he is seeing* — he names a moment, the sweep returns its full field of peers and parallels, and the pattern either densifies into something the graph can hold or honestly thins. Either result is the method working.

## PART V — THE COMPENDIUM PROGRAM: EVERY CLAIM, DECOMPOSED

The 10,000-foot ambition — the entire conspiracy corpus absorbed and mapped — is achievable under exactly one condition, and it is the condition the operator already stated: **strip the intent claims, and what remains is under-discussed history.** Formalized as a standing intake loop:

**V.1 — The claims registry.** A machine-worked queue (`claims/REGISTRY.md`) enumerating conspiracy claims systematically — from the ingested compendia, the canon, and the operator's direction. Each claim enters raw, with its provenance (who claims it, since when, in what form).

**V.2 — The decomposition.** Every claim is split into its three strata, each handled differently:
- **Documented events** — the residue that survives sourcing. These become ordinary nodes and edges, graded like everything else, and they enter the graph *as history*, stripped of the intent-frame they arrived wrapped in. This is where "under-utilized history" is minted.
- **Attributed intent** — the claim's theory of *why*. Never asserted, always attributed: carded as *what named claimant C claims, where, when*, with the claim itself graded for its sourcing. The connection/intent seam, industrialized.
- **Apocrypha** — the fabrications. These are not discarded; they are documented *as fabrications with documented lives* (a forged text is a real object with a real publication history and real consequences — the project's FICTION-ALERT machinery already knows this). The compendium maps the false claims' careers as rigorously as the true events, because the career of a false claim is itself under-discussed history — and frequently smoothing-adjacent.
- **The suppressed zone** — claims inside a documented destruction radius route to II.2: held open, suppression cited, neither confirmed nor laundered into "debunked."

**V.3 — What "proven till unprovable" means, precisely.** Each claim is pushed until every stratum has its standing: the documentable documented, the intent attributed, the fabricated exposed as fabricated, the suppressed marked suppressed, and the residue — the part no evidence can reach either way — stated plainly as the open remainder. A claim fully decomposed this way has been *completed*, not believed and not debunked. That is the compendium's unit of work, it is repeatable across the entire genre, and no other site on the internet does it.

## PART VI — AI GATES AI: THE THROUGHPUT OVERHAUL

The current gate design assumes a human in the loop at every keystone. The operator is the *director* of this system, not its bottleneck. The redesign separates two things the current architecture conflates:

**Evidence gates stay absolute and get faster.** The lint, the primary-attachment rule, the grade vocabulary, the hard sourcing rule, the erasure gate — these are the product, not process overhead. They are already machine-enforced; they scale with the loop for free.

**Human-throughput gates convert to the provisional/override pattern.** The project already invented the right mechanism in branching law C-2: *ship provisional, open an override window, promote if unopposed.* Generalize it. The two-key gate becomes two independent AI keys with adversarial briefs — a hostile-reader key (over-claim hunter) and an erasure key (under-claim hunter) — both must pass a keystone, and their disagreements route to the ruling queue instead of blocking. Operator stops become an **async ruling queue**: the loop never idles waiting for a human; it ships provisional, logs the question, keeps working the next rail, and executes reversals if a ruling overturns. The operator's role sharpens into what it actually is — veto, direction, and the naming of new territory — exercised on the operator's schedule against a machine that never stops metabolizing.

**Scale-out.** Autopilot phases parallelize by rail: ingestion runs continuously in the background; peer-sweeps batch; the claims registry is worked in standing order. The unit of progress stops being "a session" and becomes "a standing loop with a ledger."

## THE ONE-LINE VERDICT
**The project was built as a careful human wiki and it is actually an AI absorption engine with a human director — so let it be one.** Credence that accumulates instead of grades that guillotine; context priced in the year it happened, not the year it's read; a corpus digested instead of fetched; every claim in the genre decomposed until only the honest remainder is left open; exhaustion enforced by gate instead of hoped for by temperament; and a machine that never idles while the operator rules on his own schedule. Nothing on the table was ever off it — no intent beyond what authors stated, no coded collectives, everything public and sourced — and inside those two fixed walls, the entire territory is workable, to the nth degree, at machine speed.
