# The Hero with a Thousand Faces

> **WELD** · map node DP.1 · 1949

The template beneath a thousand movies — a comparative-mythology thesis that a Hollywood story department turned into a formula.

## Documented core
Joseph Campbell published *The Hero with a Thousand Faces* in **1949**, as the seventeenth title in the **Bollingen Series** (Bollingen Foundation / Pantheon Books — the Mellon-funded imprint that also carried Jung and the Eranos circle). Its claim is that the world's hero-myths are surface variants of one deep structure, the **monomyth**, decomposable into three movements across seventeen stages: **"The standard path of the mythological adventure of the hero is a magnification of the formula represented in the rites of passage: separation — initiation — return: which might be named the nuclear unit of the monomyth."** In one sentence: **"A hero ventures forth from the world of common day into a region of supernatural wonder: fabulous forces are there encountered and a decisive victory is won: the hero comes back from this mysterious adventure with the power to bestow boons on his fellow man."** Campbell took the word *monomyth* from Joyce's *Finnegans Wake*.

What is documented here is the **book and its formula**, not the truth of the cosmology behind it. Whether a single "nuclear unit" really underlies all story is contested by folklorists (Alan Dundes, Robert Segal — source-selection bias) and carried in this corpus only as an influential template (see `campbell-joseph`), never as a fact about myths.

## The industrialization — the DP.1 spine
The template's downstream is documented, and it runs through **Christopher Vogler**, not through Campbell. Working as a story analyst at **Walt Disney in 1985**, Vogler compressed Campbell's seventeen stages into a **seven-page internal memo, *A Practical Guide to The Hero with a Thousand Faces*** — his own "legendary seven-pager." It circulated hand-to-hand at Disney and then across the studios, and grew into ***The Writer's Journey: Mythic Structure for Storytellers and Screenwriters*** (1992), which reduced the schema to a **twelve-stage** development checklist and became a de-facto industry reference. This is the node's whole thesis job: a myth-theory converted into a **reusable instrument for manufacturing stories at scale**.

The on-record film adoptions are two, and only two. Vogler is **credited with story material on Disney's *The Lion King***. And **George Lucas himself acknowledged Campbell** for *Star Wars* — his own post-1977 statements, the friendship, and the **1988 *The Power of Myth* filmed at Lucas's Skywalker Ranch**, where Campbell praised the films' "valid mythological perspective." That Lucas line predates Vogler and is a separate transmission from the studio one.

## Held-open / discard
The claim that *The Matrix*, *Fight Club*, *Titanic*, *Aladdin*, *Beauty and the Beast* and the general run of blockbusters "consciously used" the template is **journalistic pattern-matching**, not documented adoption — held as *possible*, never *documented*; only *Lion King* (Vogler credited) and *Star Wars* (Lucas's own acknowledgment) are on record. Campbell's own sources — Bastian's "elementary ideas," Jung's archetypes, van Gennep's *Rites of Passage* (the literal separation–transition–incorporation scheme), Frazer, Frobenius, Rank — are the template's upstream inheritance, carried here as prose depth, not as edges. And **Brendan Gill's 1989 NYRB allegation** that Campbell was antisemitic and reactionary is attributed and contested (a defended exchange followed the same year); it is peripheral to the template argument and kept out of the documented core.

The intent must stay un-imputed (R-3): Vogler standardized a commercial story pattern — that is craft, not a plot to manage audiences. No design is claimed.

## Role in the thesis
The dispersal/pop face of the root (DP.1): where a contested theory of myth becomes a **documented craft** for the stories the grown child consumes. It is a **symptom** of a culture that wants reusable, universal, expert-optimized story — the same administrable-stock disposition the thesis tracks — not a cause of it, and not a line into any child-institution. The formula is real and documented; the reading that it is an instrument of the managed audience is the analyst's, held as `analogy`, never as lineage.
