# The Only Planet of Choice

> **WELD** · map node 4.3 · 1993

The root in the channeled idiom — a hidden council of nine steering humanity toward a chosen destiny, believed and printed.

## Documented core
*The Only Planet of Choice: Essential Briefings from Deep Space* was **channelled through Phyllis V. Schlemmer** and compiled by **Palden Jenkins** (Gateway Books, 1993) — the best-known published gathering of the transmissions attributed to **"the Nine"** (or Council of Nine, spokesperson "Tom"), the discarnate intelligence first contacted in **Andrija Puharich's** circle in the 1950s (see `lab-nine`). Its doctrine is, once again, the whole thesis in a further register: a superior hidden council that has watched over and **guides human evolution toward a planned end**, with Earth named **"the only planet of choice"** — free will granted, but stewarded from above.

Under the project's evidence bar for the esoteric current, **the book is its own primary for what it says, and the belief it records is the gradeable datum** — this is the current that says out loud what the institutions do quietly. The book, and the belief it records, stand documented.

## Held-open / discard
What the book claims about reality is **not** granted fact. That "the Nine" are real extraterrestrials or the Egyptian Ennead ("Tom" glossed as Atum) is **apocryphal, held as the text's own claim**, never asserted true. And the causal readings the material attracts — that the Nine directed *Star Trek*, or NASA, or the CIA — are **discarded under S-5**: the documented record around the sessions (Gene Roddenberry attended and wrote an unproduced screenplay; the participant network; the genre influence) is attendance and influence, **not** a channeled intelligence steering institutions. The racial-seeding cosmology in the transcripts is carried as belief-content only, never endorsed and never routed into a bloodline claim (the hard-sourcing rule).

## Role in the thesis
The esoteric-dispersal register (map-node 4.3): the latest and most literal channeled restatement of the root — administrable humanity guided toward a chosen end by a superior council. A symptom and an articulation of the century's appetite for a guiding hidden elect, printed and sold; never a cause, and never a chain into any institution.
