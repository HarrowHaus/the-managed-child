# New Atlantis

> **WELD** · map node Cap.1d · 1627

The fable in which the guardian doctrine puts on a lab coat.

## Documented core [WELD]
The island of Bensalem is administered by **Salomon's House**, a fraternity of philosopher-scientists whose spokesman states their practice plainly: they take "an oath of secrecy for the concealing of those which we think fit to keep secret." The elect decides what the public may know. Founded a generation later, the **Royal Society** took the fable as a working ideal.

## Role in the thesis
The primary the modern managerial-scientific trunk is read off — the elect re-described as expert rather than aristocrat, deciding the public's knowledge in daylight and in print.
