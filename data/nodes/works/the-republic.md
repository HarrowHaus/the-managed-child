# The Republic

> **WELD** · map node Cap.1b · c. 375 BCE

The dialogue in which the trunk is first written down.

## Documented core [WELD]
The *Republic* orders the just city by a **trained guardian class** and equips it with a **founding falsehood** — the noble lie of Book III (414b–415c), the myth of the metals, taught from childhood so that each citizen accepts the station he is held to fit. In Book V the guardians' own families are dissolved, their children raised in common, so the city may form the young directly. The management of the many by a story aimed at the child is in the source text.

## Role in the thesis
The primary the trunk is read off. A later mind that builds on it — the Royal Society off Bacon, the Fabians off their own genealogy — leaves a paper trail of reading, which is the ordinary way an idea travels and the opposite of a conspiracy.
