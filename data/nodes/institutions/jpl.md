# Jet Propulsion Laboratory

> **WELD** · map node 4.1 · 1936-

Where the occult and the rocket touch as a matter of institutional record — carried as biography, not as program.

## Documented connection
JPL grew from the Caltech **GALCIT Rocket Research Group** that **Jack Parsons**, **Edward Forman**, and **Frank Malina** formed under **Theodore von Kármán** in the mid-1930s; the Arroyo Seco working space **became the Jet Propulsion Laboratory in 1943**, and JPL has been overseen by **NASA since 1958**. Parsons — co-founder of the lab and, separately, head of a Thelemic lodge — was forced out in **1944**, when Caltech-linked colleagues made his removal a condition of the Aerojet sale, "viewing their occult activities as disreputable." The entanglement is a fact about a founder's biography, entered here as such: the man who invented the castable composite propellant also ran an O.T.O. lodge. The institution did not inherit the lodge.

## Held-open
- Popular retellings call Parsons "NASA's founder" or JPL a coven; the accurate, documented claim is narrower — Parsons co-founded the *rocket group* that became JPL, and NASA postdates him by fourteen years.
- The story that Parsons sited the lab near Devil's Gate as an occult "portal" is hedged "supposedly" in its own sourcing; kept open.

## Role in the thesis
The institutional face of the crown node: the point where the rail's occult lattice and the technical-state lattice are, by the record, the same address — with the causal reading (that the occultism *shaped* the science) left exactly where the evidence stops.
