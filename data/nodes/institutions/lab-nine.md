# Lab Nine / Round Table Foundation

> **WELD** · map node 4.3 · 1948-

Puharich's channeling operation — the documented body around the belief in a Council of Nine steering humanity.

## Documented core
Andrija Puharich founded the **Round Table Foundation of Electrobiology** in **1948** at Glen Cove, Maine, a parapsychology research outfit. In a séance there in **December 1952**, conducted through Dr. D. G. Vinod, an intelligence calling itself **"the Nine"** first spoke — the contact that would run through Puharich's work for decades. The operation later reconstituted as **"Lab Nine"** at Ossining, New York (c. 1972–78), where Puharich investigated **Uri Geller** (met 1971; tested at SRI 1972–73) and gathered the participant network around the Nine sessions — Phyllis Schlemmer, Sir John Whitmore, **Gene Roddenberry**, and the young psychics Puharich called the "Space Kids." The transcripts became, decades on, `only-planet-of-choice` (1993).

Under the project's evidence bar for the esoteric current, **Puharich's firsthand accounts are credible primary evidence for what was done and believed** — the séances, the foundation, the belief in the Nine. The operation itself is documented.

## Held-open / discard
That "the Nine" are a real discarnate/extraterrestrial council is **belief-data, held as the claim it is**, never asserted true. The causal readings are discarded under S-5: Roddenberry's involvement is documented **attendance** and a shelved screenplay, not the Nine directing *Star Trek*; and Puharich's own documented biography — his U.S. Army Edgewood Arsenal posting (1953–55) and a 1952 Pentagon paper on ESP in psychological warfare — is a **documented connection** kept rigidly apart from the **attributed, unverified** claims that the Foundation was a CIA front or the Nine a psy-op (chronological proximity only; no funding records; the sources raising it flag it unverified). No MKUltra edge is drawn on this evidence (R-3).

## Role in the thesis
The esoteric-dispersal register (map-node 4.3): the institutional face of the channeled "Nine" — a documented operation built around the belief in a hidden council guiding humanity toward a planned end. A symptom and an articulation of the century's appetite for a guiding elect; never a cause, and never a chain into any institution.
