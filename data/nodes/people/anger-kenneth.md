# Kenneth Anger

> **WELD** · map node 4.2 · 1927-2023

The documented hinge from the Parsons orbit into underground film and the rock counterculture — and a textbook case of a real connection that must not be read as a chain of cause.

## Documented connection
Anger was a self-identified **OTO member** and Crowley devotee. In his own words, he was introduced to Crowley "**by Marjorie Cameron**… She was married to Jack Parsons… After his death in 1952, Marjorie and I lived together and studied the Crowleyian teachings" — the documented tie binding his cinema to the Parsons crown. He described *Lucifer Rising* as made "in accordance with the principles of OTO," and *Invocation of My Demon Brother* (1969) carried a Moog score by **Mick Jagger** and footage of the **Rolling Stones**, with **Marianne Faithfull** later appearing in *Lucifer Rising* — the documented threads out into the music rail.

## Asserted intent — firewalled
That Anger "pushed" the Rolling Stones toward the occult is **his own framing** plus later embellishment, attributed, not documented. The sharper firewall is the Manson tail: Anger cast **Bobby Beausoleil** as Lucifer in 1967; Beausoleil later murdered Gary Hinman on Manson's orders in 1970. Anger's own account draws the line — "I fired him from the film because he was dishonest… **Two years later**, he met Manson." The casting is documented; the transmission to Manson is not — Anger fired him, and the Manson connection was independent and later.

## Held-open
Anger's and Beausoleil's accounts of a stolen *Lucifer Rising* film reel directly contradict; both kept open, neither picked.

## Role in the thesis
The rail's proof that a dense, documented web — Crowley to Cameron to Anger to the Stones to a future Manson associate — can be real at every hop and still license no directed plot. Every edge here happened; the line someone would draw *through* them did not.
