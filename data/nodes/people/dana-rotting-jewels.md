# Dana / Rotting Jewels

> **WELD** · map node 5.13 · the modern synthesizer

The named, locatable origin of the internet-era Process synthesis — reported as its source, never laundered into fact.

## Documented connection she stands on
Dana (@DanaDooDah), who runs the YouTube channel "Rotting Jewels" and the podcast *What Is "The Process"?*, describes her work as "an amalgamation of almost 2 years of active research." Two things under her are independently documented and not original to her: the Process Church's descent into **Best Friends Animal Society** (Kanab, Utah, 1991), and the **1972 Toronto grant** — the Process's Toronto chapter received **$25,900** in federal Local Initiatives Program money for its drop-in centre, a grant that **Prime Minister Pierre Trudeau** defended in the Commons on 22 March 1972 as given "in good faith to people who seemed to be engaged in doing some good work."

## Asserted intent — attributed and graded
Her throughlines are hers, stated as such and not adopted: that the Process never genuinely disbanded and Best Friends is an intelligence-operation rebrand; that the Process is an **MK-ULTRA / Louis Jolyon "Jolly" West** front; that a Crowley → Parsons → Hubbard → Process "moonchild" lineage runs through it; that the Process memoirist Timothy Wyllie is a disinformation agent; and extensions into Trudeau, the Vatican, RFK, and Candace Owens. These are conspiracy-network claims; the documented core she stands on is real, but her framing consistently runs stronger than the documentation, and the two are kept apart.

## The extensions, tested — findings, not dismissals
Each of her further threads was searched for a documented substrate rather than dismissed by association:
- **Trudeau / Canada — IN, with the layer kept straight.** The grant is documented. Dana's word for it is "**collusion**"; the record does not support that word. It shows a routine community grant defended bureaucratically, and the Process's own insider (Wyllie) framing the application as *deceiving* the government — "carefully bending the facts." Connection (the grant) is documented; intent (collusion) is her gloss. It is **deception, not collusion**, per the record. (The separately-documented Canadian MKUltra history — Cameron, the Allan Memorial — has no documented tie to the Process; joining them is a conflation, flagged on the Cameron node.)
- **Vatican — searched; no documented connection found.** The only real Rome fact is mundane (the de Grimstons rented a basement in the city while scouting a European headquarters, c. 1968); nothing ties the Holy See to the Process. Her Vatican assertion is posed as a rhetorical question with no named source.
- **Candace Owens / Scientology — searched; no documented connection found.** No reputable source ties the Owens family to Scientology.
- **RFK — searched; no documented connection found** tying her RFK material to the Process/MKUltra rail.

These are reported as findings — searched, and not found — not as dismissals by prior. Her one intent-edge onto the documented rail (the West front) is recorded above as her attributed claim and nothing more.
