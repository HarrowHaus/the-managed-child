# Alice Bailey

The Hierarchy idiom. Bailey broke from Theosophy and built her own institutional current.

## Documented core
With Foster Bailey she incorporated the organization that became Lucis Trust (1922) and founded the Arcane School (1923). Her doctrine of an initiated Hierarchy guiding humanity toward a planned unity, served by trained disciples, is rule-by-elect in the most fully esoteric idiom — and the one that built the most durable institutions.

## Held-open / discard
The organization's first name, Lucifer Publishing Company (1922), is a documented fact and a favorite of conspiracy sources; it referred to the Theosophical "light-bearer" usage and is **not** evidence of a hidden cabal. The schism from Besant's Theosophy is a documented break, cited so the current is not mistaken for a chain.

## Role in the thesis
Articulation. Bailey names the impulse — an elect administering human evolution — in its purest esoteric form, held apart from the sociology as testimony, never routed into it as cause.
