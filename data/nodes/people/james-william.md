# William James

> **WELD** · map node 6.0 · 1842–1910

The head of the academic bloodline that ends at the ranking test.

## Documented core [WELD]
James founded American psychology with *The Principles of Psychology* (1890) and, at Harvard in **1878**, supervised the doctorate of **G. Stanley Hall** — the first PhD in psychology granted in America. Hall went on to found the child-study movement and to supervise **Lewis Terman**. The loop that runs from the constitution of the child as a subject of study to its ranking by test closes inside one advisor-to-student line, and James stands at its head.

## Role in the thesis
The documented origin of the enactment rail's academic descent — advisor to student, on the record. Cited as a teaching line that leaves a paper trail, never as a directed relay.
