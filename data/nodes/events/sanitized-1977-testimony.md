# The Sanitized 1977 Testimony

> **WELD** · map node 5.8 · 1977

The too-clean account, given under oath, of a program whose own paper says otherwise.

## Documented connection
The **3 August 1977** Senate joint hearing on Project MKULTRA convened only because a FOIA request had turned up roughly **20,000 pages** of misfiled financial records that had escaped the 1973 destruction. There, Director of Central Intelligence **Stansfield Turner** testified that the events were "12- to 24-years old" and that the "CIA is in no way engaged in either witting or unwitting testing of drugs today." **Sidney Gottlieb**, testifying to the Church Committee in October 1975 under a grant of immunity, characterized the program as "probably not a high pay-off program." The account handed to Congress was of a thing old, minor, and closed.

## The smoothing
That account is the smoothing. The "failed / found nothing" characterization is contradicted by MKUltra's own internal paper — a finding that would surface only decades later, when a contractor's original 1956 report was compared with the sanitized summary that had reached the 1977 committee (see the altered West report). Nearly fifty years on, a congressional witness would put it plainly: the earlier committee "believed it had been told the truth about the program. It had not."

## Role in the thesis
The middle term of the smoothing rail: after the record is destroyed (1973), the surviving account is tidied at the point of oversight — sworn, on the record, and wrong in a direction that closes the question.
