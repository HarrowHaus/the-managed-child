# The Hard Sourcing Rule

> **PRINCIPLE** · map node Audit · —

Include beliefs; name people; never route causation through a coded collective. Symptom, not cause.

## What the rule requires
The hard sourcing rule is the project's armor and the thing that separates it from the genre it resembles. It has three demands, and the build enforces the mechanical ones. **First, the evidence moat:** no claim carries a reader-facing *documented* standing — on a node or an edge — without an attached confirming primary; uncertainty resolves *down*, not up. **Second, no coded collective:** the doctrine always resolves to **named people writing signed texts**, holding meetings that left minutes, keeping papers that survive — never to "the bankers," "the Jews," "the Illuminati," or any of the placeholder cabals the conspiracy genre routes causation through. **Third, symptom not cause, and connection is not intent:** a documented tie (two people met; one read the other; both sat on one committee) is recorded as exactly that, and is never silently upgraded into a shared design.

The rule is why the corpus can carry charged material — eugenics, the covert state, occult currents, real elite networks — without becoming the thing it studies. Where the genre needs a hidden room, the project needs a footnote.

## The exemplar
`quigley-carroll` is the rule made flesh. Carroll Quigley documented a real Anglo-American governing network — the Rhodes–Milner Round Table — **from the group's own papers**, named its members, and dated its meetings: named people, signed texts, archived records. And his documentation was then lifted by W. Cleon Skousen and Gary Allen into a controlling-world-conspiracy fantasy — an Illuminati, a bloodline — which Quigley repudiated in his own words. One figure holds both faces: the documented network the rule permits, and the coded cabal the rule refuses. The distance between them is the whole discipline.

## Held-open / discard
The rule does not forbid naming enemies who were real: the Church Committee findings, MKUltra, Northwoods, the eugenics statutes are documented, and `real-enemies` carries them at strength. The discipline is not credulity toward power, nor its mirror — reflexive debunking — but the refusal of the coded collective and the insistence on the attached primary. Where a real destruction of records is documented (the smoothing), absence is not read as proof either way; the honest resolution is to mark the seam.

## Role in the thesis
The method rail: the standing rule that governs every other node, and the reason "either way — directed or reverberating — the record reads the same" is a discipline rather than a dodge. It is what lets the project be the anti-conspiracy wiki: it shows its work, names its sources, refuses its cabals, and grades every claim it cannot yet prove.
