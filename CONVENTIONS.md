# CONVENTIONS — the discipline, as a contributor spec
*This is the spine of the project. In this repo, the grading is not commentary on the argument — **it is the argument.** A claim that isn't graded isn't done. A source that isn't named isn't a source. These rules are the merge gate: content that violates them does not enter `main`.*

---

## 1. Every edge carries a grade
- **[WELD]** — documented. A named source establishes that X met / corresponded with / cited / funded / mentored / founded / influenced Y, or that a text says what it's claimed to say. Survives a hostile reader.
- **[HYPOTHESIS]** — a resemblance, convergence, or suspected tie **not yet** documented. Legitimate to record and pursue; **never** cited as proof; never phrased as fact.
- **[FICTION-ALERT]** — a claim that circulates as fact but traces to a fictional or fabricated source. Recorded so it can never enter the foundation by accident.

**No claim ships ungraded.** If you can't grade it, it goes to `research/verification-queue.md`, not into a node's documented core.

## 2. Two registers of transmission — keep them distinct
Influence does not require coordination or a statement of intent. Ideas move by a later mind **working off** an earlier one. But *how* we know differs, and the phrasing must match:
- **WORKED-OFF** — the later party demonstrably *encountered* the earlier idea (advisor, correspondence, documented reading, attendance). → say **transmission**.
- **SAME-FIELD** — no encounter; both drew one shape from the ambient culture (e.g. Jung and Crowley). → say **reverberation / parallel emergence**, never "worked off."

"Worked off" quietly implies encounter. Use it only when the encounter is on record.

## 3. The Hard Sourcing Rule — causation, not inclusion
Nothing is excluded for the ugliness of its beliefs; a history that cut its antisemites and racialists would be false. Include the figure, the text, the belief — as **record**. What is forbidden is one causal move:
- **SYMPTOM (include & analyze):** where a repugnant view appears, read it as a **co-symptom of the root** — the rank-and-administer-humanity impulse. The thesis *explains* the pathology.
- **CAUSE (discard):** the moment "the deeper motivation" resolves to an **ethnic / financier / bloodline collective** — a coded cabal as hidden hand — the work has become the pathology it studies. Discard, don't soften.
- **Operating line:** the deeper motivation resolves to a **documented impulse held by named people** — never to a coded collective. Re-ground every claim in named institutions, people, documents.

## 4. Selection & the denominator
State the base rate out loud. Most of the managed child was built by secular actors with no esoteric tie (Mann, Cubberley, Holt, Gesell, Spock, the toy/TV complex). The esoteric current is a **high-altitude minority tributary**, not the engine. The concession comes *first*; it earns trust for everything the esoteric material then adds.

## 5. Structure ≠ intent
"Deliberate mythic structure as craft" (Vogler at Disney) is a strong, universal claim. It is **not** evidence of occult intent or content. Any specific esoteric reading of a work is a **separate claim, individually graded** — run the four-test filter (specific / deliberate / correct-to-sources / reads-one-way).

## 6. Never let B carry A's weight
Thesis A (the documented sociology) is the spine. Thesis B (the esoteric current) is the uncanny parallel — the root's self-aware *articulation*, present as testimony, not as cause. Present the two as **two testimonies to one thing**, never as a chain.

## 7. Node file format
Each node is a Markdown file with YAML frontmatter (see `data/schema/node-schema.md`). Frontmatter is the machine-readable graph; the body is the human-readable entry. `welds:` and `hypotheses:` in frontmatter must mirror the prose in the body. `status: stub | drafted | locked`.

## 8. The voice wall (essays only)
The **book** is the sober register. The **essays** are the inhabited voice. At conversion to essay prose, **every tag, grade, and node-number is stripped**; the seam between documented fact and synchromystic reading is carried *inside* the voice, never bracketed. The apparatus is scaffolding for us, never the reader.

---
*If a proposed addition can't state its grade, name its source, and say which register it's in, it isn't ready. That refusal is the product's armor — the reason this survives readers the genre usually loses to.*
