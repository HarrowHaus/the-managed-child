## THE STANDING LAWS (apply to every phase; violations fail the build)

**L1 — The No-Stubs Law (new, operator-directed).** A node routes publicly only when it meets the density bar: (a) primaries attached and claim-anchored, (b) its peer-sweep filed, (c) its claim-register rows entered, (d) its edges mechanism-classified. Anything below the bar is *unrouted and queued for absorption* — never deleted, never displayed. "Unpublish pending evidence" always means *harvest it to density and republish*, never abandon. The 20 current placeholders are the first absorption targets, not casualties.

**L2 — The Verify-the-Auditor Rule.** The Recursive Audit's corrections are claims. Each Priority-1 correction is verified against its cited source (and graded per CONVENTIONS) *before* any essay or node is rewritten on its authority. A debunk of our debunk gets the same tongs. Expected outcome: most verify; the discipline is the point.

**L3 — The Negative-Claim Law.** "No relay," "none read," "never touched," "cannot be found" are illegal phrases. The only publishable negative is *"not established in the sources checked,"* accompanied by the search universe: archives, catalogues, name/date variants actually searched, in the widening rings (published texts → correspondence/marginalia/libraries → employers/societies/intermediaries → grants/ledgers/minutes → litigation/oral histories/counterparty archives → variants and translations). An archive gap is evidence about the archive, never a universal.

**L4 — The Ethical Walls, clarified not weakened.** No routing of causation through coded collectives; no bloodline narratives; intent asserted only where the author stated it of themselves, otherwise attributed to its named claimant. **Unbundled per the Recursive Audit:** named grants, boards, ledgers, foundation minutes, and organizational funding are legitimate institutional history and are researched like everything else. Reject collective blame; inspect the ledger.

**L5 — Voice and hedge discipline.** VOICE.md remains the bar. No announcement sentences; method demonstrated, never narrated. The denominator and every structural concession is made once, architecturally, in its own essay — then every essay writes at the full strength of its evidence. Grades never move without evidence; credence anticipates, the hunt confirms, the grade flips only on the found primary.

**L6 — AI gates AI; the operator rules async.** Evidence gates are absolute and machine-enforced. Human-throughput gates convert to the provisional/override pattern (C-2 generalized): the loop ships provisional, logs the question to the ruling queue, and keeps working; the operator vetoes and directs on his own schedule. The two-key gate becomes two adversarial AI keys — the **hostile reader** (over-claim hunter) and the **eraser** (under-claim hunter, armed with L3) — whose disagreements route to the queue instead of blocking.
