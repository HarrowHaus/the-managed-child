## Added hard gates (any failure = REVISE)

1. TRANSMISSION STATUS ON EVERY CONNECTION. Every claim that two people/ideas are connected must make its status recoverable from the prose — worked-off (documented contact/absorption), same-field (parallel, no contact), or none. No connection may sit ambiguous where a reader could infer coordination that isn't claimed. Check: list every connection the essay asserts; if any one's status can't be read off the sentence, fail.

2. SURVIVES A HOSTILE READING. Read the draft as an adversary hunting for a smuggled cabal — someone who wants to screenshot one sentence to prove the project routes causation through a hidden hand. If any sentence, lifted alone, reads as "a coordinating group did this," fail it and quote it. The defense is that no such sentence exists — not that the essay disclaims it.

3. THE REFUSAL IS STRUCTURAL, NEVER STATED. This is the constraint the operator requires. The essay must NOT contain meta-commentary about what the project doesn't do — no "this is not a conspiracy," no "we never claim a cabal," no "it's important to note the distinction," no naming of the coded-collective rule. The discipline must be VISIBLE ONLY in the claims the essay declines to make and in how cleanly it assigns transmission status. If the essay explains its own method or announces its own restraint, fail it — that is over-announcement (VOICE.md Part C) at the level of the whole argument. A reader should INFER the rule from watching it hold, never be told it.

4. NO PREFACING THE POINT. Same as the screenshot notes, intensified: no sentence that sets up what the next passage will show ("what follows demonstrates…", "consider how…"). The connections land as stated facts with their status attached; the reader draws the conclusion. Cut every frame that tells the reader what to make of what's coming.

## The tension to hold
Gates 1–2 demand the discipline be airtight. Gate 3 demands it be silent. An essay that satisfies 1–2 by TALKING about transmission and cabals fails 3. The only passing essay is one that simply never makes an over-claim and states each connection's status plainly, so the honesty is demonstrated, not discussed. If the critic can't tell whether the discipline holds WITHOUT the essay explaining itself — that's the target; the prose earned it.
